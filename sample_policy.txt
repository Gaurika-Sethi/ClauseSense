R1: Employees must not share passwords in any form.
R2: All client data must be encrypted before storage or transmission.
R3: Files must not contain personal phone numbers or personally identifiable contact information.
R4: External USB devices are not allowed for transferring internal or client files.
R5: Internal credentials and access tokens must not be included in published or shared documents.
R6: Sensitive information must only be transmitted over authorized VPN channels.
